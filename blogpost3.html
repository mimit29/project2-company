Draw your Bubble Tea

January 10th, 2017

Bubble Tea House Lunar New Year special event! Submit a drawing of yours to us through a post on Instagram with the theme My bubble tea. General Rules:

  1. One submit per user
  2. Drawing must be creation of your own
  3. Be Creative!
